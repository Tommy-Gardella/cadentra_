Common Questions

Quick Answers.

If you don't find what you're looking for, just send us a message above.

When does Cadentra launch? ▾
We're currently in early development. Waitlist members will be the first to know — and the first to get access. Sign up on the homepage to stay in the loop.
Is Cadentra only for advanced runners? ▾
Not at all. Cadentra is built for runners at every level — from first-timers training for a 5K to experienced athletes chasing ultras. The AI adapts to where you are, not where it thinks you should be.
How is this different from a generic training plan? ▾
Generic plans are static — they don't know your schedule, your injury history, or how you're actually feeling. Cadentra is dynamic. It builds around your real life and adjusts in real time based on what you tell it.
Will there be a free tier? ▾
Pricing details are still being finalized. Waitlist members will receive founding-member pricing, which will be significantly discounted from the standard rate. Stay tuned.
Do you integrate with Garmin, Apple Watch, or Strava? ▾
Integrations are on the roadmap. We want Cadentra to meet you wherever your data lives — wearables, Strava, Apple Health, and more. We'll share updates as those features are ready.
