Our Story

The Coach You Never Had — Until Now.

Cadentra was born from a simple conviction: elite-level coaching shouldn't be reserved for elite-level runners. Every person who laces up deserves a plan that actually fits their life.

Founder & CEO — Cadentra

Founder Story

It Started With One Too Many Overtraining Injuries.

Like a lot of runners, I followed plans I found online — generic 12-week programs built for an average runner who doesn't exist. The mileage jumps were too aggressive for my schedule. The rest days fell on my busiest workdays. And when life happened, the plan didn't adapt — it just fell apart.

After a stress fracture sidelined me for four months, I started asking a different question: why does coaching have to be static? Real coaches adjust. They talk to you. They know when to push and when to back off.

Cadentra is the answer to that question. It uses AI to do what a great human coach does — listen, adapt, and build something that actually works for you. Not a template. Not a spreadsheet. A real plan, in real time.

We're early. The product is still being shaped. But the mission is clear: make world-class running coaching accessible to every runner, at every level, chasing every goal.

Our Mission

Three Things We Believe.

01

Training Should Fit Your Life

A plan that ignores your schedule, your sleep, and your stress isn't a plan — it's a liability. Cadentra builds around you, not around an imaginary average runner.

02

Coaching is a Conversation

Static PDFs can't tell you when to cut a run short or how to taper for humidity. Real coaching is dynamic, responsive, and always in your corner.

03

Every Runner Deserves Better

Premium human coaching costs $200/month and is out of reach for most runners. We're changing that. World-class guidance shouldn't require a world-class budget.
