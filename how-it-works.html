The System

From First Run to Finish Line — Here's How It Works.

Cadentra takes you from "where do I even start" to race-ready with a five-step system powered by AI and built around your real life.

1
📋
Step One

Onboarding

Answer a short intake — your current fitness level, weekly availability, recent race history, injury background, and your goal race. Cadentra doesn't ask for more than it needs.

In under 5 minutes, it has everything it needs to build a plan that's genuinely yours.

2
⚡
Step Two

AI Plan Generation

Cadentra's coaching engine builds a fully periodized training block — long runs, tempo sessions, easy days, cross-training, and recovery — structured around your race date and your life.

No cookie-cutter templates. The plan accounts for your current fitness, your available hours, and your target time — and it's ready in seconds.

3
📅
Step Three

Weekly Calendar

Your training lives in a clean weekly view — every workout laid out with distance, pace targets, and workout type. No interpretation needed. You see exactly what to run today, tomorrow, and the rest of the week.

Each week is a building block, and Cadentra always shows you where you are in the bigger picture.

4
💬
Step Four

Adjust via Chat

Life happens. You missed a workout. Your knee is acting up. You got an extra race on the calendar. Just tell your coach — in plain English — and it adapts.

Cadentra's chat interface understands context. It doesn't just answer questions — it updates your plan in real time based on what you tell it.

5
📈
Step Five

Track Progress

Log every run and watch your training load, pace trends, and weekly mileage build over time. Cadentra flags when you're progressing well and when to back off.

More than just numbers — it gives you the insight to understand what's working and why.
